# Pain Points and Objection Handlers

**Last updated:** 2026-06-05

---

## Pain Points by Product Line

### Optics
- Cisco-branded SFPs cost 5-10x the equivalent third-party transceiver
- Compatibility anxiety ("will it work with my gear?")
- Supply chain delays on OEM optics

**Discovery question:** "What are you paying for SFPs today, and are they Cisco-branded or third-party?"

---

### DWDM / SmartOptics
- Wave extension on backbone is expensive and slow (hyperscalers consuming transport supply)
- 100G deals becoming uncompetitive because of transport costs
- Lead times on Ciena/Nokia gear stretching 6-12 months
- Cell site backhaul bandwidth pressure

**Discovery question:** "Are you seeing lead time issues on transport gear? What's your current 100G wave cost look like?"

---

### Compute / DIMMs
- OEM memory pricing is 30-50% above third-party with manufacturer warranties
- Server refresh backlogs from COVID supply chain still playing out
- AI workloads driving unexpected DIMM demand

**Discovery question:** "When did you last refresh your DIMM inventory? Are you buying direct from Dell/HP or through distribution?"

---

### TPM
- Cisco SmartNet renewals increasing 10-15% per year
- OEM support on EoL gear is extremely expensive or unavailable
- Multi-vendor environments make OEM support impractical (different contracts, portals, SLAs)

**Discovery question:** "When does your SmartNet renew? Have you ever run a TPM comparison?"

---

### Storage
- NetApp renewal costs increasing significantly
- Capacity gaps between refresh cycles
- Budget constraints forcing "make do" decisions on aging arrays

**Discovery question:** "Are you on a NetApp renewal cycle? What's your support spend look like annually?"

---

## Objection Handlers

### "We only use OEM-approved vendors."
"Totally understand. We're a Dell and HP authorized partner, and for TPM we're Gartner-recognized — same coverage, same SLAs, 40-60% less. Worth a quick comparison on your next renewal?"

### "We had a bad experience with third-party optics."
"That's fair — compatibility issues are real with some vendors. SmartOptics is manufacturer-direct and we send samples before you commit to anything. What gear are you running them on?"

### "We're happy with Cisco SmartNet."
"Makes sense — until renewal. When does yours come up? A lot of our customers said the same thing right up until they saw the quote."

### "We're in a freeze / no budget right now."
"Good to know. When does your next budget cycle start? I'll put a note to follow up then — no point talking now if the timing's off."

### "We already have a vendor for this."
"Who are you using? I ask because we often complement rather than replace — especially on the optics side where we can run alongside your existing Cisco gear."

### "Send me some information."
"Happy to. What would make it worth 5 minutes of your time — optics, compute, or TPM? I'll send something targeted rather than a brochure."

### "We don't work with new vendors without a formal RFP."
"Understood. We work with a lot of enterprise procurement teams. Want me to send our vendor registration info so you have it when the next refresh comes up?"
