### Where Elemental Damage Comes From

**Elemental Armament supports** are the backbone — on Charged Staff (II), Falling Thunder (I), Shattering Palm (I), and Ice Strike (II). In PoE2, Elemental Armament adds elemental damage to skills based on the weapon's physical damage, so the staff's phys damage does double duty.

**Three Heralds each add a flat elemental layer:**
- Herald of Ash — fire damage based on physical hits
- Herald of Thunder — lightning damage + storm on kill
- Herald of Ice — cold damage + shatters

**Inherently elemental skills:**
- Ice Strike — base cold
- Falling Thunder — base lightning
- Charged Staff — base lightning

**Short version:** High phys staff → Elemental Armament converts it to elemental on most skills → three Heralds amplify and proliferate each element for ignite/shock/freeze chains.

## FGKorbyn21 Act 1 Zone Breakdown (Lvl 1–5)

### Zone Progression
- **Clearfell** → Abandoned Stash (Uncut Skill Gem), kill Beira for +10% Cold Res
- **Grelwood** → Hunt Areagne the Forgotten Witch (Tier 1 Support + Medium Flasks)
- **The Red Vale** → Best EXP zone, click Refined Arms racks for weapon upgrades, kill The Rust King for Runed Skull Cap. League mechanic drops Lvl 2 Uncut Skill Gem (use for Frost Bomb)
- **The Grim Tangle** → Rush to exit. League mechanic drops Lvl 3 Skill Gem (use for Wing Blast)

### Skill Priority (Act 1, Lvl 1–5)
1. **Falling Thunder** — Main boss + large pack DPS (uses Power Charges)
2. **Frost Bomb** — Elemental Exposure (lowers resistances for more Falling Thunder damage)
3. **Killing Palm** — Culls low HP enemies → grants Power Charges (1 normal, 2 rare, 3 unique)
4. **Contagion** — Early pack clear via Chaos DoT spread (drop at Ogham Farmland)
5. **Quarterstaff Strike** — Rage builder when no Power Charges

### Support Gem Priority
1. Rage I → Quarterstaff Strike (up to 30% more DPS)
2. Rapid Attacks I → Quarterstaff Strike (generates Rage faster)
3. Fist of War I → Falling Thunder (20% more damage every 6s)
4. Perpetual Charge → Falling Thunder (25% chance to not lose charges)

### Gameplay Loop
1. Apply Contagion to pack → kill one → DoT spreads to all
2. Finish low HP enemies with Killing Palm → bank Power Charges
3. Unleash Falling Thunder on big packs/bosses with charges
4. Quarterstaff Strike to build Rage when no charges
5. Wolf Form (Weapon Set 2 / Talisman) for 30% move speed between pulls
6. Drop Frost Bomb on bosses for Elemental Exposure

**Boss tip:** Don't dump Power Charges in first 5 seconds — GGG built in a boss damage reduction window. Use it to build Rage, apply Shock, and drop Frost Bomb first.

### Gear Priority (Early)
Quarterstaff (upgrade to Long QS ASAP) → Rings (Added Damage) → Gloves (Added Damage) → Body/Helm/Boots (Life + Armor + Resists)  
**Prioritize Cold Resistance early** for the Count Geonor fight.

## NickTew Campaign Leveling Method

**Core loop:** Place Frozen Locus on pack → Glacial Cascade into it = freeze explosion  
**Big boss multipliers:** Shock (up to 90%) + 30% cold from Freezing Mark + 30% cold from Ice Bite II + 425% more from Glacial Cascade consuming freeze

**Gem acquisition order:**
- Lvl 1: Glacial Cascade (from Renly), Frost Bomb (Clearfell Abandoned Stash)
- Lvl 2: Frozen Locus (Mud Burrow Devourer), Glacial Cascade upgrade (Red Vale)
- Lvl 3: Glacial Cascade (Rust King), Pounce (Crowbell)
- Lvl 5: Tempest Flurry (Manor Ramparts League Mechanic) — for boss shock only
- Tier 1 Supports: Concentrated Area → Glacial Cascade, Cold Attunement × 2, Ice Bite I → Frozen Locus, Shock → Tempest Flurry

**Transitions to:** Whirling Assault at level 41 → early maps → eventual crit swap
