# Winning Email: Smartoptics for Fiber/Carrier Service Providers

**Saved:** 2026-05-19
**Product:** Smartoptics / optical networking
**Vertical:** Regional fiber-based service providers, carriers, ISPs, broadband operators
**Use case:** 100G wave cost and lead time challenges, Cell Site Backhaul (CSBH), transport gear shortage
**Format:** Situation / Solution / Result
**Tone:** Consultative, peer-level, confident — not salesy

## When to Use
- Prospect is a regional fiber operator, ISP, or broadband provider
- They likely deal with backbone wave extension costs or long lead times on transport gear
- Competitors (hyper scalers, neo cloud) are consuming transport gear supply
- Good for senior IT/network engineering leads at companies like Astound, Breezeline, Zirrus, Surf Internet, Synergy Fiber, TruVista, and similar

## The Email

My guess is that you are experiencing a situation we are seeing quite commonly in the carrier market, and we fully expect it to be getting worse. The great news is that we have a way to tackle it, and we are doing so quite successfully with some of your peers/competition. Here's a brief rundown.

**SITUATION:** Astound, in all likelihood, has shared the experience that we see with many other regional fiber based service providers, namely extending waves off the backbone can present significant cost and, more dramatically, lead time challenges (the hyper scalers and now neo cloud providers are gobbling up transport gear). As a result, "no brainer" "slam dunk" 100G wave deals get overburdened with costs and become uncompetitive and/or the delivery times are beyond unacceptable.

**SOLUTION:** OSI has partnered with Smartoptics, a Scandinavian based manufacturer of game-changing disruptive optical networking gear that is open, can complement existing systems (Infinera, Ciena etc), is SIGNIFICANTLY lower cost, requires less power and space, reduces noise and most importantly, with OSI handling the logistics, can be in our customers' hands in days/weeks!

**RESULT:** Our service provider customers (perhaps your competitors) are winning way more deals, simplifying customer turn-ups and realizing revenue MUCH faster. As one of our largest customers has said, "Smartoptics has become our hammer and EVERYTHING is a nail!"

On top of that, the Smartoptics portfolio also includes a killer solution for dealing with the increasing bandwidth demands for Cell Site Backhaul (CSBH).

If this sounds familiar, we believe we could provide a similar impact to your business. Would love to have an exploratory conversation with [NAME] to see if you are seeing similar challenges. As part of that, we would love to share with your team this "lego box" of solutions so you can see where they may fit into your solution set. With AI driving demand for high bandwidth transport and data center interconnect, we believe these are essential solutions for the times.

## Notes
- Replace [NAME] with the prospect's first name or their boss/decision maker
- "lego box" line is a strong visual — keep it
- The competitor/peer social proof angle ("your peers/competition") is the hook — lead with it
- Works well as Email 1 or Email 3 in a sequence for fiber/carrier verticals
